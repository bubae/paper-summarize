2. Modeling

2.1 Voxtral Codec (~300M params)

24 kHz mono waveformмқ„ 12.5 Hz н”„л Ҳмһ„мқҳ 37к°ң discrete tokens (1 semantic + 36 acoustic)мңјлЎң м••м¶•н•ҳлҠ” convolutional-transformer autoencoderмқҙлӢӨ.

Waveform Autoencoder

"Patchified" waveform л°©мӢқ: 24 kHz inputмқ„ 240 sample лӢЁмң„лЎң non-overlapping patchлЎң л¶„н• н•ҳм—¬ 100 Hz inputмқ„ мғқм„ұ.

Encoder: Causal convolution (kernel 7) вҶ’ 1024-dim projection вҶ’ 4 encoder blocks. к°Ғ block:

  • 2-layer causal self-attention transformer (sliding window attention, ALiBi positional bias, QK-norm, LayerScale $\epsilon=0.01$). Window sizes: $16 \rightarrow 8 \rightarrow 4 \rightarrow 2$
  • Causal CNN layer (stride 2лЎң downsample, мІ« 3 block). 4лІҲм§ё blockмқҖ stride 1 + 1024вҶ’292 dim projection
  • мҙқ 8Г— downsampling: 100 Hz вҶ’ 12.5 Hz, мөңмў… 292-dim latent

Decoder: Encoderмқҳ м—ӯкө¬мЎ°. 292-dim вҶ’ 1024-dim causal CNN вҶ’ 4 blocks (transposed CNN 2Г— upsample + 2-layer causal self-attention transformer) вҶ’ causal convolution (kernel 7) вҶ’ 240 samplesлЎң unpatch.

Representation Quantization вҖ” Hybrid VQ + FSQ

292-dim latentмқ„ л‘җ componentлЎң л¶„лҰ¬:

  • Semantic component (256-dim): Learned VQ (Vector Quantization) with codebook size 8192. Training мӢң 50% нҷ•лҘ лЎң VQ м Ғмҡ©, лӮҳлЁём§Җ 50%лҠ” unquantized pass-through
  • Acoustic component (36-dim): к°Ғ dimensionмқ„ tanh вҶ’ 21 uniform levelsлЎң FSQ (Finite Scalar Quantization). Training мӢң dither-style: 50% FSQ, 25% uniform noise ($1/L$, $L=21$), 25% unquantized

мҙқ bitrate: $12.5 \times (\log_2 8192 + 36 \times \log_2 21) \approx 2.14$ kbps.

Semantic Token Learning вҖ” ASR Distillation

кё°мЎҙ л°©лІ•мқҖ self-supervised representations (HuBERT л“ұ)мқ„ distillн•ҳм§Җл§Ң мқҙлҠ” "phonetic"м—җ к°Җк№Ңмҡҙ нҶ нҒ°мқ„ мғқм„ұн•ңлӢӨ. Voxtral CodecмқҖ ##supervised ASR model (frozen Whisper)м—җм„ң distill##н•ҳм—¬ лҚ” effective semantic representationмқ„ мғқм„ұн•ңлӢӨ.

Whisperмқҳ л§Ҳм§Җл§ү decoder layer hidden states $h_l$м—җ лҢҖн•ҙ, post-VQ semantic embedding $z_f$лҘј linearly projectн•ҳм—¬ alignment. Whisperмқҳ cross-attention weightsм—җм„ң soft alignment matrix $A \in \mathbb{R}^{L \times F}$лҘј м¶”м¶ң (word-level timestampмҷҖ к°ҖмһҘ мһҳ correlationн•ҳлҠ” attention head subsetмқ„ DTWлЎң мӢқлі„):

$$\mathcal{L}_{\text{ASR}} = 1 - \frac{1}{L} \sum_{l=1}^{L} \frac{\tilde{z}_l \cdot h_l}{\|\tilde{z}_l\| \|h_l\|}, \quad \tilde{z}_l = \sum_{f=1}^{F} A_{l,f} \, z_f \quad (1)$$

$\tilde{z}_l$мқҖ codec frame $f$мқҳ semantic embeddingмқҳ attention-weighted sumмңјлЎң, Whisperмқҳ $l$лІҲм§ё decoder tokenм—җ м •л ¬лҗң codec representationмқҙлӢӨ. мқҙ м„Өкі„лЎң ##мҷёл¶Җ forced alignerлӮҳ paired transcript м—ҶмқҙлҸ„ text-aligned semantic tokenмқ„ н•ҷмҠө##н•  мҲҳ мһҲлӢӨ.

Adversarial Training

Multi-resolution discriminator: 8 STFT sizes (2296, 1418, 876, 542, 334, 206, 126, 76). к°Ғ discriminatorлҠ” real/reconstructed audioлҘј hinge lossлЎң л¶„лҘҳ. $L_1$-based feature-matching lossлҘј standard GAN generator loss лҢҖмӢ  мӮ¬мҡ© (evolving discriminator featuresк°Җ лҚ” discriminativeн•ң signal м ңкіө):

$$\mathcal{L}_{\text{feature}}(\boldsymbol{x}, \hat{\boldsymbol{x}}) = \frac{1}{MN} \sum_{m=1}^{M} \sum_{n=1}^{N} \|D_n^m(\boldsymbol{x}) - D_n^m(\hat{\boldsymbol{x}})\|_1 \quad (2)$$

Training Objective

$$\alpha \mathcal{L}_{\text{feature}} + \beta \mathcal{L}_{\text{ASR}} + \gamma \mathcal{L}_{\text{L1}} + \gamma \mathcal{L}_{\text{STFT}} + \delta \mathcal{L}_{\text{commit}} \quad (3)$$

$\alpha=1.0$, $\beta=1.0$, $\gamma=0.9999^t$ (exponential decay), $\delta=0.1$. Reconstruction losses ($\mathcal{L}_{\text{L1}}$: waveform L1, $\mathcal{L}_{\text{STFT}}$: STFT magnitude L1)лҠ” exponential decayлЎң н•ҷмҠө мҙҲкё°м—җ bootstrapн•ҳкі , нӣ„л°ҳм—җлҠ” adversarial signalмқҙ м§Җл°°н•ҳлҸ„лЎқ н•ңлӢӨ.

[Figure 3 м„ӨлӘ…]: Voxtral Codec м•„нӮӨн…ҚмІҳ. Encoder: Patch вҶ’ Causal Conv вҶ’ Transformer Block Г— 4 (Strided Causal Conv нҸ¬н•Ё) вҶ’ Linear $\sigma$ (split to 256+36) вҶ’ VQ (semantic, codebook 8192) + FSQ (acoustic, 21 levels Г— 36). Decoder: м—ӯкө¬мЎ°. Semantic tokenм—җлҠ” frozen Whisper ASR modelм—җм„ңмқҳ soft-aligned distillation lossк°Җ м¶”к°Җ. Adversarial + Reconstruction lossesк°Җ м „мІҙм—җ м Ғмҡ©.

2.2 Decoder Backbone (Ministral 3B)

Ministral 3B м•„нӮӨн…ҚмІҳмқҳ autoregressive decoder-only transformer. мһ…л Ҙ мӢңнҖҖмҠӨ: [voice reference audio tokens] + [text prompt tokens] вҶ’ м¶ңл Ҙ: [semantic audio tokens] + <EOA>.

  • к°Ғ audio frameмқҖ 37к°ң discrete tokens (1 semantic + 36 acoustic)лЎң н‘ңнҳ„
  • к°Ғ codebookмқҖ лҸ…лҰҪм Ғмқё embedding lookup tableмқ„ к°Җм§Җл©°, н”„л Ҳмһ„лі„лЎң н•©мӮ°н•ҳм—¬ лӢЁмқј embedding мғқм„ұ
  • Linear headк°Җ hidden state $h$лҘј semantic codebook vocabulary (8192 + <EOA>)м—җ лҢҖн•ң logitsлЎң нҲ¬мҳҒ, standard cross-entropy lossлЎң н•ҷмҠө
  • Acoustic token мҳҲмёЎмқҖ flow-matching transformerм—җ мң„мһ„ (Section 2.3)
  • FM transformerмқҳ float-valued outputмқҖ 21 FSQ levelsлЎң discretizeлҗҳм–ҙ лӢӨмқҢ AR stepмқҳ discrete token inputмңјлЎң мӮ¬мҡ© вҶ’ ##fully discrete token interface мң м§Җ##

2.3 Flow-Matching Transformer

к°Ғ AR generation stepм—җм„ң, decoder backboneмқҳ hidden state $h$лҘј мЎ°кұҙмңјлЎң acoustic token (36-dim continuous vector)мқ„ мғқм„ұн•ҳлҠ” bidirectional 3-layer transformer.

Flow-matching formulation: Gaussian noise $x_0$м—җм„ң acoustic embedding $x_1$мңјлЎңмқҳ velocity fieldлҘј лӘЁлҚёл§Ғ. $0 \leq t \leq 1$мқҳ function evaluation stepsм—җм„ң:

  • Input: $h$ (hidden state from backbone), $t$ (current step, sinusoidal embedding), $x_t$ (current acoustic state, $x_t \in \mathbb{R}^{36}$)
  • к°Ғ мһ…л ҘмқҖ лі„лҸ„ projection layer (activation scaleмқҙ лӢӨлҘҙлҜҖлЎң)
  • DiT-style AdaLNліҙлӢӨ separate projectionмқҙ мҡ°мҲҳ (ablation нҷ•мқё)

Training: Hidden stateлҘј 10% dropoutн•ҳм—¬ "unconditional" modeling вҶ’ inference мӢң CFG (Classifier-Free Guidance) м Ғмҡ©:

$$v_t = \alpha v_\theta(x_t, t, h) + (1-\alpha) v_\theta(x_t, t, \emptyset) \quad (4)$$ $$x_{t-\Delta t} = x_t - v_t \cdot \Delta t \quad (5)$$

$\Delta t = 1/8$, $\alpha = 1.2$. ##CFGк°Җ FM transformerм—җл§Ң м Ғмҡ©лҗҳлҜҖлЎң, decoder backbone м „мІҙм—җ CFGлҘј м Ғмҡ©н•ҳлҠ” кІғліҙлӢӨ кі„мӮ°лҹүмқҙ нҳ„м ҖнһҲ м ҒлӢӨ##. 8 NFE (Number of Function Evaluations)к°Җ кё°ліё, 16 NFEм—җм„ң marginal improvementл§Ң мһҲмңјлҜҖлЎң 8мқ„ мұ„нғқ.

лҢҖм•Ҳ м•„нӮӨн…ҚмІҳмҷҖмқҳ л№„көҗ:

  • MaskGIT: 36 acoustic codebook position + conditioning = per-frame sequence length 38 (vs FMмқҳ 3). Human evalм—җм„ң expressiveness м—ҙм„ё
  • Depth Transformer (Moshi л°©мӢқ): 36 autoregressive decoding steps/frame (vs FMмқҳ 8 NFE). Quality, compute, latency лӘЁл‘җ м—ҙм„ё

3. Training

3.1 Pretraining

Voxtral Mini TranscribeлЎң pseudo-labeledлҗң paired audio + transcript мӮ¬мҡ©. к°Ғ н•ҷмҠө мғҳн”Ң: $(A_1, T_2, A_2)$ вҖ” $A_1$мқҖ voice reference, $T_2$лҠ” transcript, $A_2$лҠ” мғқм„ұ target.

  • $A_1$, $A_2$: лҸҷмқј нҷ”мһҗмқҳ single-speaker segments, л°ҳл“ңмӢң temporally adjacentлҠ” м•„лӢҳ
  • Maximum duration: $A_1, A_2$ к°Ғк°Ғ 180мҙҲ, $A_1$ мөңмҶҢ 1мҙҲ
  • Voice prompt мөңм Ғ кёёмқҙ: 3-25мҙҲ (мһҘмӢңк°„ лҢҖнҷ”мқҳ long-tail нҠ№м„ұ кі л Ө)
  • <next> special tokenмңјлЎң $A_1$кіј $T_2$ мӮ¬мқҙлҘј, <repeat> tokenмңјлЎң $T_2$мҷҖ $A_2$ мӮ¬мқҙлҘј кө¬л¶„

Loss: $A_2$мқҳ tokensм—җл§Ң loss кі„мӮ°. Two-part loss:

  1. Semantic loss ($\mathcal{L}_{\text{semantic}}$): Semantic tokenм—җ лҢҖн•ң cross-entropy
  2. Acoustic loss ($\mathcal{L}_{\text{acoustic}}$): Conditional flow-matching objective
$$\mathcal{L}_{\text{acoustic}} = \mathbb{E}_{t \sim \mathcal{U}[0,1], x_0 \sim \mathcal{D}, x_1 \sim \mathcal{N}(0,1)} \|v_\theta(x_t, t) - u_t(x_t | x_1, x_0)\|_2^2 \quad (6)$$ $$u_t(x_t | x_1, x_0) = x_1 - x_0 \quad (7)$$

Implementation details:

  • Backbone: Ministral 3Bм—җм„ң мҙҲкё°нҷ”. FM transformer, audio codebook embedding lookup-tables, output projectionмқҖ random initialize
  • Text-embedding layersлҠ” freeze (Voxtral Mini Transcribeмқҳ low-frequency text tokenм—җ лҢҖн•ң robustness)
  • VADлЎң speech м—ҶлҠ” н”„л Ҳмһ„м—җ lower loss weight, extremely long silenceм—җ loss weight 0
  • LLM-based transcript rewriteлЎң normalized vs un-normalized text robustness нҷ•ліҙ (мҳҲ: "5 - 4" vs "five minus four")

3.2 Direct Preference Optimization (DPO)

WER, speaker similarity к°ңм„ мқ„ мң„н•ң post-training. ##Hybrid discrete-continuous м„Өм •м—җ DPOлҘј м Ғмҡ©н•ҳлҠ” мөңмҙҲмқҳ мӢңлҸ„##:

Semantic DPO

Standard DPO objective with uniform weights:

$$\mathcal{L}(\theta) = -\mathbb{E}_{t \sim \mathcal{U}(0,1), x^w, x^l} \log \sigma \left(-\beta \left(\Delta_\theta(x^w, x^l, t) - \Delta_{\theta_{\text{ref}}}(x^w, x^l, t)\right)\right) \quad (8)$$

Flow-based Acoustic DPO

Acoustic tokenмқҙ flow-matchingмңјлЎң мғқм„ұлҗҳлҜҖлЎң, flow-matching objectiveлҘј preference objectiveлЎң ліҖнҷҳ (Ziv et al., 2025 кё°л°ҳ):

$$\Delta_\theta(x^w, x^l, t) = \|v_\theta(x_t^w, t) - u_t(x_t^w | x^w)\|_2^2 - \|v_\theta(x_t^l, t) - u_t(x_t^l | x^l)\|_2^2 \quad (9)$$

Autoregressive м„Өм •м—җ л§һкІҢ, к°Ғ нҶ нҒ°мқҙ лӢӨлҘё $\mathbf{t}$лҘј мғҳн”Ңл§Ғн•ҳлҸ„лЎқ мҲҳм •:

$$\Delta_\theta(x^w, x^l, \mathbf{t}^l) = \sum_{i=1}^{N_w} \|v_\theta(x_{i,\mathbf{t}_i}^w, \mathbf{t}_i) - u_{i,\mathbf{t}_i}^w\|_2^2 - \sum_{i=1}^{N_l} \|v_\theta(x_{i,\mathbf{t}_i}^l, \mathbf{t}_i) - u_{i,\mathbf{t}_i}^l\|_2^2 \quad (10)$$

Length normalization (winner кёёмқҙлЎң лӮҳлҲ„кё°)мқҖ instabilityлҘј мң л°ңн•ҳм—¬ мӮ¬мҡ©н•ҳм§Җ м•ҠмқҢ. $\beta_{\text{semantic}} = 0.1$, $\beta_{\text{acoustic}} = 0.5$, LR $8 \times 10^{-8}$.

DPO лҚ°мқҙн„° мғқм„ұ: Rejection-sampling pipeline вҖ” single-speaker voice samples + н•©м„ұ text prompts (Mistral Small CreativeлЎң мғқм„ұ) вҶ’ pretrained checkpointлЎң multiple samples мғқм„ұ вҶ’ WER, speaker similarity, loudness consistency, UTMOS-v2, LM judgeлЎң winner/loser pair кө¬м„ұ. Pretraining objectiveмҷҖ DPO lossлҘј кІ°н•©н•ҳм—¬ high-quality speechм—җм„ң 1 epoch н•ҷмҠө. Synthetic dataлЎң мһҘмӢңк°„ н•ҷмҠөн•ҳл©ҙ robotic speechк°Җ лҗҳлҜҖлЎң 1 epochмқҙ мөңм Ғ.

  1.
